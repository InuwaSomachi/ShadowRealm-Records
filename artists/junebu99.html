The Ghetto Oracle

Emerging from concrete labyrinths where flickering streetlights illuminate forgotten truths, Junebu99 resurrects 90s boom-bap spirituality through a prism of modern urban decay. His gravel-voiced poetics ride crackling vinyl samples and trap hi-hat exorcisms, chronicling street-corner prophecies and corner-store revelations.
