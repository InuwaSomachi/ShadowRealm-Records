Hailing from forgotten alleyways where streetlights flicker like dying stars, Junebu99 resurrects 90s boom-bap spirituality through cracked smartphone speakers. His verses - equal parts survival guide and eulogy - ride distorted soul samples and trap percussion that echoes through housing project corridors. The self-taught "sidewalk scribe" documents hood realities with the precision of a crime scene investigator, his flow alternating between gravel-throated warnings and tender neighborhood eulogies. Recent works feature collaborations with underground producers, blending cassette tape hiss with blockchain-era paranoia.
