JUNEBU99

Street Scriptures Author

The undying scribe of concrete psalms, Junebu99 wanders eternal through urban mazes, his verses etched in alleyway mortar and subway dust. This self-crowned "ghetto griot" spins raw tales over decaying boom-bap loops and trap artillery. His lyrics—part survival guide, part obituary—float on beats forged from stolen car stereos and police siren samples. Followers report finding hidden tracks on corrupted USB drives left in abandoned lots.
